A Beginner's Guide to ENHYPEN

About

ENHYPEN is a South Korean boy group under BELIFT Lab, a subsidiary label under HYBE. The group consists of 7 members: Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki (View Profiles). They debuted on November 30th, 2020 with the mini album, "BORDER: DAY ONE", and the title track, "Given-Taken".

ENHYPEN Fandom Name: Engene

Group Name Meaning: Their name is a combination of the words "en" and "hyphen" minus the "h", in which the hyphen connects different words to create new meanings. The members of ENHYPEN will connect, discover, and grow together.

How ENHYPEN was Formed

Enhypen was formed through a survival show, I-Land, in 2020 which was produced by Mnet and organized by BELIFT Lab. I-Land was a show where twenty-three contestants had to train and compete with each other to win the top seven spots to debut. These contestants lived together in two remote complexes, isolated from society, for 113 days. The concept of the show is to have the contestants' skills evaluated by judges and contestants by voting (applies to the first part of the show). The second part only allows the judges and audience to vote for who will debut in the final line-up. Throughout the show, contestants will be eliminated based on the evaluations and votes.
