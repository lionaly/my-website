A Beginner's Guide to ENHYPEN

About

ENHYPEN is a South Korean boy group under BE:LIFT Lab, a subsidiary label under HYBE. The group consists of 7 members: Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki. Their profiles can be found here. They debuted on November 30th, 2020 with the mini album, "BORDER: DAY ONE".

How They Were Formed

Enhypen was formed through a survival show, I-Land, in 2020. I-Land was a show where contestants had to train and compete with each other to win the top spots to debut.
