A Beginner's Guide to ENHYPEN

About

ENHYPEN is a South Korean boy group under BE:LIFT Lab, a subsidiary label under HYBE. The group consists of 7 members: Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki (View Profiles). They debuted on November 30th, 2020 with the mini album, "BORDER: DAY ONE", and the title track, "Given-Taken".

How They Were Formed

Enhypen was formed through a survival show, I-Land, in 2020 which was produced by Mnet and organized by BE:LIFT Lab. I-Land was a show where contestants had to train and compete with each other to win the top spots to debut.
